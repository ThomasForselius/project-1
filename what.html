What is FriendFeud?
It's fun! That's what it is!

- Scroll down for history and rules -

The idea

FriendFeud is a set of real life games and events with the purpose of bringing people together.

This webpage is the digital hub that connects those people and the results of the games, as well as show a road map of what events are in the pipeline.

History

Thomas, the creator of FriendFeud, is studying webdesign and wanted a real life project for his design course, and this game was the product.

The basis for this game was a drunken brainstorm between friends that may have started small and soon got out of hand - as many ideas do when alcohol is induced.
In this case, it was a good thing!
